Method: 1) 将调度问题建模为不安定多臂老虎机(RMAB)框架；2) 证明纠缠请求调度具有索引性；3) 推导所有请求-年龄对的Whittle索引闭式解；4) 将内存约束调度问题转化为0-1背包问题并用动态规划求解；5) 提出两种基于修改Whittle索引的低复杂度贪心策略。

Result: 成功证明了纠缠请求调度的索引性，获得了Whittle索引的闭式表达式，并设计了基于动态规划和贪心策略的调度算法，能够有效优化平均服务请求数。

Conclusion: 提出的Whittle索引方法为量子交换机内存受限调度问题提供了有效的解决方案，两种低复杂度贪心策略在实际部署中具有应用价值，为量子网络资源管理提供了新思路。

Abstract: We consider a quantum switch with a finite number of quantum memory registers that aims to serve multipartite entanglement requests among $N$ users. We propose scheduling policies that aim to optimize the average number of requests served per unit time by efficiently utilizing the switch's available memory. To measure the performance of the scheduling policies, we employ the newly introduced metric of age of entanglement establishment (AoEE). We formulate the scheduling problem in a restless multi-armed bandit (RMAB) framework. We show that the scheduling of entanglement requests is indexable. Subsequently, we find a closed-form expression of the Whittle index for all possible request-age pairs. By modeling the Whittle index of each request as its reward and its cardinality as its cost, we formulate the memory-constrained scheduling problem as a $0$-$1$ knapsack problem and solve it via dynamic programming. Furthermore, we consider two low-complexity sequential greedy policies that leverage two different modified Whittle indices.
