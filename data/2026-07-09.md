And for two classes of these four-weight self-orthogonal linear codes, we determine the parameters of their dual codes. As applications, we construct two classes of LCD codes and a class of quantum codes. In particular, we prove that there exists a class of these LCD codes whose dual codes are almost optimal LCD codes according to the sphere packing bound, and a class of quantum codes are AMDS according to the quantum Singleton bound.
