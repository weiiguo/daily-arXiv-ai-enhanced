### [1] [Function-Correction with Optimal Data Protection for the General Hamming Code Membership](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14023)
*Adityawardhan Yadava,Anjana A. Mahesh,Swaraj Sharma Durgi*

Main category: cs.IT

TL;DR: 本文研究针对汉明码隶属函数的单纠错功能校正码，提出了系统化构造方法，将最小化距离-2码字对问题转化为最大割问题，并发现对于偶数n，最优设计等价于bent布尔函数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为汉明码隶属函数这一重要函数类设计高效的单纠错功能校正码，建立理论框架并寻找最优奇偶校验分配策略。

Method: 建立了有效奇偶分配的充要条件；证明距离-3码字图是连通二部图；将最小化距离-2码字对转化为最大割问题；利用图的最小特征值对应的特征向量求解最优分配；对于偶数n，将问题转化为Walsh系数的矩优化问题，并证明bent函数达到下界。

Result: 实现了最小距离为2的系统化SEFCC构造；对于偶数n，推导出紧下界并由bent函数实现，建立了最优SEFCC设计与bent布尔函数之间的精确联系。

Conclusion: 本文为汉明码隶属函数的最优SEFCC设计提供了完整理论框架，揭示了编码理论与布尔函数优雅的联系，bent函数在偶数n情况下是最优的。

Abstract: This paper investigates single-error-correcting function-correcting codes~(SEFCCs) for the $[2^n-1,\,2^n-1-n,\,3]$-Hamming code membership function~(HCMF) for general $n\geq 2$. Necessary and sufficient conditions for valid parity assignments are established, and the distance-$3$ codeword graph is shown to induce a connected bipartite structure for all $n\geq 2$, which is exploited to develop a systematic SEFCC construction achieving the largest possible minimum distance of~$2$. A novel framework is then developed that reduces the minimization of distance-$2$ codeword pairs to a max-cut problem on the distance-$4$ graphs of the two partite sets. Eigenvectors corresponding to the minimum eigenvalue of these graphs are shown to directly yield optimal parity assignments. We reduce the problem of finding these eigenvectors to an optimization problem involving moments of the Walsh coefficients of a related function, which we solve for even~$n$ by deriving a tight lower bound shown to be attained by bent functions, establishing a precise connection between optimal SEFCC design and bent Boolean functions.

### [2] [Antenna Tilt Failure Detection and Estimation via Integrated Sensing and Communications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14510)
*Samed Kesir,Batuhan Kaplan,Emre Arslan,Ahmet Faruk Coskun*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于ISAC的免传感器天线倾角故障检测与估计框架，利用环境静态杂波作为几何锚点，监测杂波热图中的系统性增益变化，实现窄波束通信系统中天线物理偏差的自主检测与修复。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 窄波束通信系统对物理未对准高度敏感，容易因天线倾斜导致性能下降，而传统方法需要外部传感器进行检测，成本高且部署复杂。本文希望利用ISAC技术实现免传感器的自主检测与估计。

Method: 将环境中的静态杂波作为几何锚点，通过监测杂波热图中的系统性增益变化来检测天线倾角故障。利用标准5G NR帧结构，结合两种不同波形进行天线倾角的精确检测与估计。

Result: 数值结果表明该框架能够有效检测和估计天线倾角故障，具备实现自主、自愈网络维护的潜力，无需外部传感器。

Conclusion: 本文提出的ISAC框架为无线通信网络的自主运维提供了新思路，免传感器设计降低了部署成本，自愈能力提升了网络可靠性。

Abstract: This paper addresses the critical sensitivity issue of narrow-beam communication systems to physical misalignments and exploits the potential of Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC) technology to propose a sensor-free antenna tilt failure detection and estimation framework. The proposed methods utilize environmental static clutter as geometric anchors to monitor systematic gain shifts in clutter heat maps. The proposed methods are introduced for precise antenna tilt detection and estimation using the standard 5G NR frame structure and two different waveforms. Numerical results show the potential of the proposed framework to enable autonomous, self healing network maintenance without the need for external sensors.

### [3] [Transmit Beamforming for High-Rate Underwater Acoustic Communications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14919)
*Diego A. Cuji,Andrew C. Singer,Milica Stojanovic*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文研究了水下声通信中基于角度的波束赋形策略，利用传播场中稳定的几何结构（尤其是主导路径）来降低对完整信道信息的依赖，并通过SPACE和MACE数据集验证了该方法在数据检测均方误差和误码率方面的有效性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 水下声通信的发射波束赋形需要预先精确了解信道信息，但实际中信道估计常因反馈延迟和信道变化而带有噪声或过时，因此需要减少对完整信道知识的依赖性。

Method: 提出基于角度的波束赋形策略，利用传播场中随时间相对稳定的几何结构（特别是存在的主导路径）进行发射波束赋形，无需完整的信道状态信息。

Result: 使用SPACE和MACE数据集进行实验，结果表明该方法在数据检测均方误差和误码率方面表现有效。

Conclusion: 基于角度、利用稳定主导路径的波束赋形策略能够有效降低对完整信道知识的依赖，在水下声通信中具有实用价值。

Abstract: Transmit beamforming for underwater acoustic communication is challenging because it requires perfect knowledge of the channel to the receiver in advance. In practice, channel estimates must be learned through feedback and are often noisy or outdated because of feedback delay and channel variation. In this paper, we investigate angle-based beamforming strategies for a single-user link that reduce dependence on full channel knowledge by exploiting stable components of the geometric structure in the propagation field. In particular, we focus on scenarios in which there exists a dominant path that remains relatively stable over time, making it a suitable candidate for transmit beamforming. Experimental results using the SPACE and MACE data sets demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method in terms of data-detection mean-squared error and bit error rate.
